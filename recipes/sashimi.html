Tuna Sashimi

One of the many delicious ways to create tuna sashimi

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tbsp mirin
  • 3 tsp sake
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tbsp tamari
  • 1/2 tsp dashi granules
  • 1 daikon radish
  • 1 fresh peeled ginger
  • 1 sushi grade tuna steak

Preparation

  1. Add mirin and sake to small pot and bring to a boil.

  2. Turn off heat and add soy sauce, tamari and dashi granules.

  3. Mix well and let this come to room temperature.

  4. With a sharp knife, square off daikon. Then slice into very thin sheets, either with a mandoline or a knife.

  5. Stack sheets and slice again into very thin sticks. Toss them all into a bowl of ice water and make sure they're all separated. Gently wring out and dry daikon, then arrange some on each plate.

  6. Use a fine grater for ginger, then mound and create little cones for each plate.

  7. Cut tuna into a block, cut thin slices against the grain. Slice thin strips, then arrange on plate over daikon. Garnish plate if preferred.
