California Roll

Simple and delicious California sushi roll

Ingredients

  • 2 2/3 cup calrose rice
  • 3/4 cup crab meat
  • 1/3 cup Japanese mayonnaise
  • A quarter cucumber
  • 1 avocado
  • A quarter lemon
  • 3 nori sheets
  • 1/8 cup toasted white sesame seeds
  • Masago

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, mix the crab meat with Japanese mayonnaise

  2. Peel the cucumber, remove the seeds and cut into strips that are the same length as the nori sheets.

  3. Peel the avocado, remove the pit, cut into thick slices and then squeeze the lemon juice over them.

  4. Cut the nori sheet into two halves and place on a bamboo mat with the shiny side facing down.

  5. Spread 1 cup of rice evenly onto the sheet before sprinkling some of the sesame seeds on the rice

  6. Turn the nori sheet over so the rice is laying on the bamboo mat and place the crab meat, cucumber and avocado across the bottom of the nori sheet.

  7. Holding the bottom of the bamboo mat, roll forward whilst keeping the filling tucked in. Cut the sushi into equally sized pieces with a sharp knife.

  8. Use your hands to spread masago evenly across the top of each sushi roll.
